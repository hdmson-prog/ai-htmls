Who We Are

A bearing manufacturer
with an operator's mindset

We build wheel hub bearing solutions for customers that care about fitment accuracy, stable lead times, and measurable durability in the field. Our teams work across sourcing, process control, validation, and global account support so each project moves from drawing to delivery with less friction.

From high-volume aftermarket programs to application-specific development for OEM and fleet use, Nexus Bearings focuses on repeatability. That means traceable materials, disciplined quality systems, and a product strategy shaped by real operating conditions.

Technical Overview

AX-4320 Gen III combines a compact hub layout with stabilized internal geometry for low friction and reduced noise. It suits front-axle passenger applications where ride quality and durability both matter.

The assembly uses a sealed bearing pack, optimized preload, and corrosion-resistant surfaces to preserve performance through urban stop-start use and long-range highway runs.

Load Rating 28.4 kN
Bore Size 40 mm
Seal Type IP67
Noise Class Class 1

Applications and Notes

Recommended applications

Passenger cars, compact crossovers, and light-duty EV platforms that prioritize wheel stability, predictable steering feel, and efficient packaging.

Performance notes

The low-drag seal system reduces heat generation at cruising speed, while the geometry is tuned to keep wheel-end noise controlled over extended service intervals.

Service guidance

Recommended for OEM programs requiring long maintenance windows. Refer to the datasheet for mounting torque, lubrication, and fitment tolerances.

Our Company

Precision-Engineered
for Generations

Founded in 1989, AXION Precision Bearing Group has grown from a regional manufacturer into one of the world's most respected wheel hub bearing specialists. Our state-of-the-art facilities span three continents, supplying tier-1 automotive OEMs and aftermarket distributors with bearing assemblies of unrivalled quality and consistency.

35+ Years Heritage
Three decades of precision manufacturing excellence passed down through our engineering culture.
ISO/TS 16949 Certified

Global Distribution

Zero-Defect Policy

Material Science

Steel That Endures
Every Condition

We source only premium SAE 52100 bearing steel and GCr15 alloy, processed through our proprietary carburising and heat-treatment protocols to achieve optimal hardness, fatigue life, and corrosion resistance far beyond industry standards.

  • Proprietary heat treatment to 62–65 HRC
  • Ultra-clean steel with ≤ 10 ppm oxygen

Product Details

Built for stable steering response and long-life operation

The AXH-3842 combines a compact hub geometry with integrated sensor support and a sealed bearing package. It is designed for OE replacement, aftermarket catalogues, and private-label programmes where fitment consistency matters.

  • Precision-machined hub body for tighter fit consistency.
  • Dual seal arrangement to keep out moisture and road contamination.
  • Balanced geometry for smoother rotation and reduced vibration.
  • Manufactured under automotive quality management systems.
Integrated ABS ring Supports wheel speed sensing without extra assembly steps.

Our Beginning

Where Vision Meets Artistry

Founded in 2009 by award-winning photographer Alexandre Moreau, LUMIERE Studio was born from a singular vision: to create photography that transcends the ordinary. What began as a solo venture has evolved into a full-service creative studio renowned for its distinctive aesthetic and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our name, LUMIERE—French for "light"—reflects our philosophy. We believe that great photography is fundamentally about understanding and manipulating light to reveal the extraordinary within the everyday. Every project we undertake is an opportunity to explore the interplay between shadow and luminescence, between technical precision and artistic intuition.

Over fifteen years, we've built a reputation for delivering work that doesn't just document moments—it elevates them. Our portfolio spans luxury fashion editorials, intimate portraits, grand wedding celebrations, and transformative brand campaigns. Yet regardless of the project scope, our approach remains constant: artistry grounded in technical mastery, vision tempered by empathy.

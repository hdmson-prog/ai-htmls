Frequently Asked

Common Questions

What is your average turnaround time for photos?

Our standard turnaround is 2-3 weeks for initial edits. Rush delivery (5-7 business days) is available for an additional fee. We discuss timeline preferences during the initial consultation.

Do you offer package deals?

Yes, we offer customized packages tailored to your specific needs. Whether you need a single session or a comprehensive campaign, we can create a package that fits your vision and budget. Contact us for personalized pricing.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the initial results?

We stand behind our work. If you have concerns about the initial edits, we'll work with you to make adjustments. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we won't consider a project complete until you're thrilled with the results.

Can I use the photos for commercial purposes?

Usage rights are included in your service agreement and depend on the project type. Editorial and campaign photos come with commercial use rights. We'll clearly outline what's included in your contract before we begin.

Do you offer retouching services?

Absolutely. Retouching is included in all our packages. For extensive retouching beyond what's standard, we offer additional retouching services for an additional fee. We can discuss your specific needs during consultation.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance for standard availability. Peak seasons (spring and fall) may require longer lead times. Emergency bookings are considered on a case-by-case basis depending on our schedule.

What's your cancellation policy?

Bookings cancelled 30+ days in advance receive a full refund. 15-30 days receives 50% refund. Less than 15 days is non-refundable but can be rescheduled. We understand life happens—just give us as much notice as possible.

Do you provide location scouting?

For larger projects, yes. We can recommend stunning locations that align with your vision or scout custom locations. Location scouting fees are discussed as part of your project planning.

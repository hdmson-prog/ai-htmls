As a specialized wheel hub bearing manufactory, we operate as a Tier 1.5 supplier to the global automotive industry. Our facility combines advanced CNC machining centers with automated assembly lines to deliver consistent, precision-engineered components.

We serve both OEM and aftermarket segments with a focus on long-term partnerships, technical collaboration, and reliable supply. Our position bridges the gap between Tier 1 scale and specialized manufacturing flexibility.

Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our standard minimum order is 50 kilograms per SKU. For custom blends and private label projects, minimums may vary based on complexity and packaging requirements.

Do you offer samples?

Yes. We provide sample sets to qualified trade partners following an initial consultation. Samples are shipped at no charge for serious inquiries.

What certifications do you hold?

We maintain ISO 22000:2018, USDA Organic, EU Organic, Fair Trade, and Rainforest Alliance certifications. Full documentation is available upon request.

Can you develop custom blends?

Yes. Our master blender works directly with partners to develop bespoke formulations. The process typically requires 6-8 weeks from brief to final approval.

Do you offer private label services?

Yes. We provide complete private label solutions including formulation, packaging consultation, and fulfillment. Brand confidentiality agreements are standard.

What are your lead times?

Standard orders ship within 2-3 weeks. Custom blends and private label projects require 8-12 weeks depending on specifications and packaging requirements.

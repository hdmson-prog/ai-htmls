Our Commitment
Est. 1897

A lineage of precision

In the mist-veiled hills of our founding estate, a commitment was made: to honor the leaf, respect the process, and never compromise on quality.

Over 125 years, our manufactory has remained in family stewardship, evolving from a single processing shed to one of the world's most respected tea production facilities. We do not follow trends. We set standards.

Our archives hold formulations passed down through four generations. Our modern facility employs the precision of contemporary engineering. Together, they create teas that speak to both tradition and excellence.

We serve luxury hotels, specialty distributors, and private label partners who understand that exceptional tea begins long before it reaches the cup.

Process

From field to finished tea

Our manufactory combines time-honored methods with precision engineering. Each stage is monitored, measured, and mastered.

01

Selection

Only leaves meeting our exact specifications enter the facility. Each harvest is inspected by our master graders.

02

Withering

Controlled environment withering reduces moisture content to precise levels, preparing leaves for optimal oxidation.

03

Oxidation

Temperature and humidity are managed to the degree. This stage defines character, complexity, and final flavor profile.

04

Firing

Heat arrest oxidation at the ideal moment. Our kilns ensure uniformity across every batch, preserving aroma and integrity.

05

Grading

Mechanical and manual sorting separate leaf grades. Only consistent, premium lots proceed to blending or packaging.

06

Quality Assurance

Laboratory analysis and sensory evaluation confirm every parameter. Our standards exceed international certification requirements.

Portfolio

Signature offerings

Estate Reserve Black

Full-bodied orthodox black tea with malty depth and copper liquor. Our flagship offering for over a century.

Imperial Green

Delicate spring-harvest green with vegetal sweetness and pale jade infusion. Minimally processed, maximally refined.

High Mountain Oolong

Semi-oxidized tea with floral complexity and lingering finish. Sourced from elevations above 1,500 meters.

Moonlight White

Rare white tea with silvery tips and subtle honeyed notes. Hand-selected, naturally withered, exceptionally pure.

Heritage Breakfast Blend

Robust morning blend of Assam and Ceylon. Formulated in 1923, unchanged since. Full, satisfying, dependable.

Aged Pu-erh

Fermented tea with earthy complexity and smooth, dark liquor. Aged in controlled conditions for a minimum of seven years.

Jasmine Silver Needle

Premium white tea scented with night-blooming jasmine. Delicate, aromatic, labor-intensive. A study in restraint.

Custom Formulations

Bespoke blends developed in collaboration with our master blenders. Tailored to specification, brand, and purpose.

Responsibility

Stewardship, not extraction

About Us

A tea house shaped by mountain origin and quiet precision.

MUSHA TEA curates ceremonial and estate green teas for buyers, retailers, and hospitality programs that value provenance, craft, and composure in the cup.

Home / About Us
Our Story

We work where editorial taste meets sourcing discipline.

Our portfolio is built around green teas that speak clearly of place. We source from mountain gardens and shaded estates that can support ceremonial quality, consistent lot standards, and long-term trade relationships.

Rather than expanding into broad commodity catalogues, we stay narrow and intentional. That keeps the collection coherent and gives our partners a stronger product story to present to their own customers.

Focus

Three priorities guide every selection.

  • Distinct mountain or estate identity
  • Reliable ceremonial and premium service quality
  • Packaging and formats suited to trade presentation
Timeline

How the archive took shape over time.

From a narrow sourcing project to a trade-facing tea archive, each stage sharpened the collection and the kind of partners we serve.

2012

Initial mountain sourcing visits

The work began with direct visits to Fujian and Zhejiang gardens to identify growers capable of premium consistency and stronger traceability.

2016

First ceremonial-focused collection

The portfolio narrowed around ceremonial-grade and service-ready green teas with clearer positioning for specialty retail and tea rooms.

2020

Export and hospitality expansion

MUSHA began supporting hotels, premium counters, and international partners with tailored assortments, sampling, and service guidance.

Today

A refined archive for modern trade

The current archive balances ceremonial depth, estate identity, and presentation-ready formats for buyers who need both quality and a stronger story.

3 Core sourcing regions
14yr Export and buyer support
180+ Retail and hospitality partners
How We Work

Small archive, strong point of view.

We support boutiques, hotels, tea rooms, and premium retail counters with a concise product line, tasting guidance, and service recommendations. The goal is not volume for its own sake, but a sharper assortment with better storytelling and repeatable quality.

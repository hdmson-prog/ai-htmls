Launch Update

MUSHA introduces the new ceremonial archive for retail and hospitality buyers.

A closer look at how the latest archive narrows the assortment around ceremonial-grade service, stronger sourcing clarity, and trade-ready formats.

Home / News / Ceremonial Archive Launch
March 2026 4 min read

The new MUSHA archive reduces noise and puts more attention on the teas buyers actually need help presenting: ceremonial matcha, service-ready green teas, and small reserve lots with stronger product framing.

For hospitality and specialty retail partners, the shift means a more focused range with cleaner assortment logic. Instead of treating every tea as a separate pitch, the collection now groups around use case, service style, and customer expectation.

"A sharper archive makes both the tea and the story easier to carry into service."

MUSHA Trade Team

The archive also introduces clearer sample pathways for buyers. Ceremonial teas, estate greens, and reserve lots can now be evaluated within a more structured shortlist, which improves internal tasting and speeds up ranging decisions.

For existing partners, the update is less about volume than clarity. Packaging formats, assortment language, and service recommendations now align more closely with how tea is actually sold and served in premium environments.

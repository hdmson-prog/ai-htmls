Ceremonial Grade

Jade Mist Matcha

A stone-milled Uji matcha with dense emerald color, round sweetness, and the quiet depth expected from ceremonial service.

Home / Products / Jade Mist Matcha
Uji, Kyoto

Ceremonial matcha for koicha and slow whisking rituals.

Jade Mist Matcha is built from first-harvest tencha leaves grown under shade before being stone milled into a soft, luminous powder. The cup opens with sweet cream, steamed greens, and a restrained chestnut finish.

$48 30 g caddy
Texture Silken and dense
Notes Sweet pea, cream, chestnut
Harvest First flush, spring lot
Service Usucha or koicha
Request samples Back to archive
Profile

Clarity, sweetness, and a steady finish.

The shade period encourages higher amino acid retention, giving the liquor a richer body without sacrificing lift. In the bowl, the texture stays creamy and the finish remains composed rather than aggressive.

This lot is intended for buyers and drinkers who want a ceremonial tea that reads polished and approachable, but still carries enough structure for formal preparation.

Brewing Notes

  • 2 g matcha to 60 ml water
  • Water temperature: 75 to 80°C
  • Whisk until the foam is fine and even
  • For koicha, reduce water and increase leaf

Sourcing

  • Shade-grown Uji tencha
  • Stone-milled in small batches
  • Packed in oxygen-protected tins
  • Reserved for ceremonial-grade allocation

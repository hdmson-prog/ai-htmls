About Precision Automotive

A global manufacturing partner trusted by OEMs for precision, reliability, and scale.

Our Story

Engineering Critical Systems for the World’s Leading Automakers

Founded in 1992, Precision Automotive has grown from a single machining facility into a global manufacturer of mission-critical components. Our teams deliver precision parts for powertrain, chassis, and electric mobility platforms across every major automotive region.

We combine advanced manufacturing with relentless quality standards, enabling OEMs to reduce risk, accelerate launches, and scale production with confidence.

CNC Precision Machining

5-Axis CNC Machining

Advanced multi-axis machining centers capable of producing complex geometries with tolerances down to ±0.005mm. Our facilities operate 24/7 with automated quality inspection at every stage.

  • 200+ CNC machining centers
  • Automated tool management
  • Real-time process monitoring
Die Casting Operations

High-Pressure Die Casting

State-of-the-art aluminum and magnesium die casting for lightweight structural components. Integrated with robotic handling and automated finishing lines for maximum efficiency.

  • 1,200-4,000 ton presses
  • Vacuum-assisted casting
  • In-line heat treatment
Forging & Forming

Precision Forging

Hot and cold forging capabilities for high-strength chassis and powertrain components. Material utilization optimized through near-net-shape manufacturing processes.

  • Open-die & closed-die forging
  • Material flow simulation
  • Grain structure optimization
Assembly & Testing

Automated Assembly Lines

Fully automated assembly and testing facilities with 100% inline quality verification. Traceability systems ensure complete documentation from raw material to finished product.

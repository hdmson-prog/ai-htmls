Precision-machined gears for ICE and hybrid drivetrains with high torque durability.

Our transmission gears are engineered with optimized tooth geometry, advanced heat treatment, and precision grinding for reduced noise and wear. Each gear set meets OEM-grade durability testing and high-volume production requirements.

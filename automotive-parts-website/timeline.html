1992 - 2026

Our Journey

Major milestones that expanded our manufacturing footprint and technical capabilities.

1992

Precision Automotive founded in Michigan with a focus on high-tolerance drivetrain machining.

1998

Secured first Tier-1 supplier contract and expanded CNC capacity to support global OEMs.

2004

Opened European manufacturing campus dedicated to powertrain and transmission systems.

2009

Launched advanced die casting division to support lightweight aluminum components.

2014

Standardized APQP and digital quality monitoring across all global facilities.

2018

Expanded chassis and suspension product lines with forged aluminum control arms.

2021

Opened first EV systems facility with battery enclosure and motor housing lines.

2024

Global SAP rollout connected production reporting and quality traceability.

2026

Achieved carbon-neutral manufacturing and increased EV platform capacity by 40%.

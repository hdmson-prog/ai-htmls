§1 Our foundation

Established 1947 — Stuttgart, Germany

Founded on the conviction that industrial materials must outlast the structures they support.

Meridian was founded in the immediate post-war reconstruction period, when the need for reliable, high-specification industrial materials was not merely a commercial proposition but a structural imperative. Our founders — engineers from the precision tooling tradition of Baden-Württemberg — established the company on a single principle: that every component leaving the facility should perform without question for the full intended service life.

That founding discipline has guided seven decades of evolution. Through the expansion into export markets in the 1960s, the acquisition of specialised metallurgical facilities in the 1980s, and the integration of digital process control systems in the 2000s, the core values have remained unchanged: precision, accountability, and the long view.

Today, Meridian operates as an integrated industrial group — with manufacturing, quality assurance, logistics, and technical support operating as a single coordinated system. Our clients are not purchasing products. They are entering a manufacturing partnership built on institutional trust.

"The quality of an industrial component is only revealed across time. We engineer for that revelation." — Heinrich Brauer, Founder, 1947

About Precision Metals

Three decades of engineering excellence in copper and brass manufacturing

Manufacturing Excellence Since 1995

Precision Metals has been at the forefront of copper and brass component manufacturing for over 30 years. What began as a small precision machining workshop has evolved into a global supplier serving 85+ countries with state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our success is built on a foundation of technical expertise, advanced manufacturing technology, and a customer-centric approach. We serve industries ranging from HVAC and plumbing to automotive and aerospace, delivering components that meet the most demanding specifications.

With ISO 9001:2015 certification and compliance with international standards including EN, ASTM, and DVGW, we ensure every product leaving our facility meets the highest quality benchmarks.

Our Mission

To deliver precision-engineered copper and brass components that exceed industry standards, enabling our customers to build reliable, efficient systems that stand the test of time. We are committed to continuous innovation, sustainable manufacturing practices, and building long-term partnerships based on trust and technical excellence.

To be the global benchmark for precision metal component manufacturing, recognized for our unwavering quality standards, technical innovation, and customer-centric approach.

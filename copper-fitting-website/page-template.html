Multi-Stage Inspection Process

Our quality control methodology follows a systematic approach that identifies and corrects potential issues at the earliest possible stage:

1. Incoming Material Inspection

Every raw material shipment undergoes thorough verification before entering production:

  • Material Certificates Review: Verification of mill certificates and material composition documentation
  • Spectroscopy Testing: Elemental analysis to confirm alloy composition meets specifications
  • Dimensional Check: Measurement of raw material dimensions to ensure conformance
  • Visual Inspection: Surface examination for defects, oxidation, or contamination
  • Traceability Marking: Batch numbering system for complete material traceability

2. In-Process Quality Control

Throughout the manufacturing process, our quality control technicians perform regular checks:

  • First Article Inspection: Complete dimensional verification of the first part in every production run
  • Statistical Process Control: Real-time monitoring of critical dimensions using SPC techniques
  • Periodic Sampling: Regular inspection of parts during production to ensure consistency
  • Process Parameter Monitoring: Continuous tracking of machining speeds, feeds, and temperatures
  • Tool Wear Monitoring: Systematic tool inspection and replacement to maintain precision

CMM Inspection and Quality Control Laboratory

3. Final Inspection & Testing

Before shipment, all products undergo comprehensive final inspection:

Dimensional Verification

  • CMM Inspection: Coordinate measuring machine scanning for critical dimensions with ±0.005mm accuracy
  • Optical Measurement: Non-contact measurement systems for complex geometries
  • Surface Profile Analysis: Profilometer testing to verify surface finish requirements
  • Thread Inspection: Thread gauges and optical comparators for threaded components

Functional Testing

  • Hydrostatic Pressure Testing: Pressure testing to 1.5x working pressure for leak detection
  • Flow Rate Testing: Verification of flow characteristics for fittings and valves
  • Torque Testing: Thread strength and assembly torque verification
  • Sealing Performance: O-ring compression and sealing effectiveness testing

Material Verification

  • Hardness Testing: Rockwell or Vickers hardness measurement to verify heat treatment
  • Tensile Testing: Mechanical property verification on sample parts
  • Corrosion Resistance: Salt spray testing for protective coatings
  • Metallography: Microscopic examination of material structure when required

Advanced Inspection Equipment

Our quality control laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art metrology equipment:

CMM Systems

Three coordinate measuring machines with 0.005mm accuracy for precise dimensional verification

Optical Systems

High-resolution optical comparators and measuring microscopes for complex geometries

Spectrometers

Optical emission spectrometers for material composition analysis and alloy verification

Pressure Test Rigs

Automated hydrostatic testing systems capable of testing to 400 PSI with leak detection

Quality Documentation & Traceability

Complete documentation accompanies every order:

  • Material Certificates: Mill certificates showing material composition and heat lot numbers
  • Inspection Reports: Detailed dimensional inspection data for critical features
  • Test Results: Pressure test results, hardness values, and functional test data
  • Certification of Conformance: Statement of compliance with customer specifications
  • Batch Traceability: Unique batch numbers linking products to raw materials and processing

We understand that different applications have unique quality requirements. Our team works closely with customers to implement:

Copper Press Fitting 22mm

High-purity copper press fitting engineered for plumbing and HVAC systems. Leak-proof O-ring sealing technology reduces installation time by 60% compared to traditional soldering methods.

Lead-Free NSF Certified ISO 9001

Key Specifications

  • Material C12200 Copper (99.9% purity)
  • Size 22mm
  • Pressure Rating 16 bar (232 PSI)
  • Temperature Range -20°C to 120°C
  • Connection Type Press Fit with O-ring
  • Standards EN 1254-7, DVGW, NSF/ANSI 61

Product Description

Our Copper Press Fitting 22mm represents the pinnacle of modern plumbing and HVAC connection technology. Manufactured from high-purity C12200 copper with 99.9% material purity, this fitting delivers exceptional performance and longevity in demanding industrial and commercial applications.

Advanced Press-Fit Technology

The integrated O-ring sealing system eliminates the need for traditional soldering, brazing, or threading. Using specialized press tools, installations are completed in seconds rather than minutes, significantly reducing labor costs and project timelines. The cold-connection method also eliminates fire hazards associated with torch work, making it ideal for occupied buildings and sensitive environments.

Material Excellence

Sourced from certified suppliers with complete material traceability, our copper undergoes rigorous spectroscopy verification before production. The C12200 alloy composition ensures optimal thermal conductivity (385 W/m·K) and corrosion resistance, critical for long-term reliability in water distribution and refrigeration systems.

Quality Assurance

Every fitting undergoes multi-stage inspection including dimensional CMM scanning (±0.01mm tolerance), hydrostatic pressure testing to 24 bar (1.5x working pressure), and visual inspection. Our ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing process guarantees consistent quality across millions of parts annually.

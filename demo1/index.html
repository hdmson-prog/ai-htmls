Heritage & Legacy

A lineage shaped by cultivation, refinement, and measured growth.

Founded in the highland tea corridors of East Asia, the manufactory traces its origins to a family concern established for careful leaf preparation and long-standing export relationships. Over decades, that early discipline matured into a corporate house serving international buyers with consistency, discretion, and technical care.

Today, our work remains grounded in the same principles: respect for origin, measured production, and accountability at every stage of the manufacturing chain. The result is a tea program that speaks softly, yet with authority.

Manufactory & Craftsmanship

Traditional judgement supported by exacting process control.

01

Leaf Selection

Harvest windows, elevation, varietal, and leaf integrity are reviewed before intake, ensuring raw material begins at the appropriate standard.

02

Withering & Resting

Controlled airflow and monitored intervals stabilize the leaf and preserve aroma development without compromising structure.

03

Fixation & Rolling

Traditional technique is paired with calibrated equipment to protect character while maintaining repeatable manufacturing outcomes.

04

Drying & Finishing

Moisture targets, sorting tolerances, and sensory review are applied with precision to support shelf stability and cup clarity.

05

Quality Assurance

Batch sampling, traceability, and export documentation form part of a measured control system suited to professional procurement standards.

06

Scalable Fulfilment

Dedicated planning across seasonal production, blending, and packing allows scale without compromising the refinement expected of a premium house.

Brand Story

Tea shaped by patience, place, and a respect for the leaf.

Verdant Mist began with a simple belief: exceptional green tea should carry the calm of its landscape into every cup. From early spring harvests to export-ready finishing, each batch is guided to preserve clarity, freshness, and origin character.

Our team works across plantation relationships, artisanal selection, and contemporary packaging development so buyers can source with both emotional resonance and commercial confidence.

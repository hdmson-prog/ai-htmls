Chunmee Green Tea

Grade: Special 41022 Origin: Zhejiang, China

Our signature Chunmee is characterized by its thin, curved leaves resembling a "precious eyebrow." This Special Grade selection offers a clean, brisk liquor with a subtle sweetness and a classic toasted finish.

The Details

Crafted with Precision

Brewing Guide

  • Temperature: 80°C (176°F)
  • Time: 2–3 minutes
  • Quantity: 3g per 200ml

Sensory Notes

A pale gold infusion with a clean, high aroma. The taste is lively and vibrant, with a refreshing edge that lingers on the palate.

Sustainability

Sourced from rain-fed highlands under strict quality control. Our facility is certified for export to EU and US markets.

Tea Gardens Zhejiang Mountain Mist

Origin Story

Roots in Tradition

Grown in the rolling hills of the Pan'an county, our Chunmee tea is harvested at the peak of the season when the leaves are most tender. The processing follows a multi-step wither, roll, and pan-fire technique that has been refined over generations.

About Atelier

Built on Quiet Excellence

Our Story

Designing for the Way Modern Homes Live

Atelier Home began with the belief that appliances should contribute to architecture, not compete with it. For more than four decades we have developed kitchen, laundry, cooling, and air care systems that balance technical precision with a calm visual presence.

Our teams work across engineering, industrial design, and material development to shape products that feel considered in use and durable over time. The result is a collection built for clients who care about detail.

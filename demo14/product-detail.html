Integrated e-Axle 3-in-1 Module

Our flagship propulsion system integrates a high-efficiency inverter, permanent magnet motor, and advanced transmission into a single, compact unit. Engineered for silicon carbide (SiC) compatibility and extreme power density.

System Overview

The Gen-3 e-Axle is designed for modern 800V EV architectures, offering modular performance scaling from 150kW to 350kW. Its ultra-compact footprint allows for superior vehicle packaging and increased storage capacity or battery volume. Every unit is manufactured in our ISO 26262 certified facilities to ASIL-D safety standards.

Peak Power Up to 350 kW
Input Voltage 400V - 800V DC
Peak Torque 4,500 Nm (at axle)
Weight 82 kg (complete)
Safety Standard ISO 26262 ASIL-D

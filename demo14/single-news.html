The 800V e-Axle Evolution

  • Home

  • 800V e-Axle Update
August 24, 2026 | Detroit, USA

Advancing High-Voltage Propulsion
Architectures for Next-Gen EVs

Global AutoSystems (GAS) today announced a significant breakthrough in electric drivetrain technology with the unveiling of its third-generation 800V e-Axle module.

The new architecture, which integrates a custom-designed silicon carbide (SiC) inverter, permanent magnet synchronous motor, and high-efficiency primary reduction gear, sets a new industry benchmark for power density. Reaching over 4.5 kW/kg, the GAS Gen-3 system provides OEMs with unprecedented packaging flexibility.

"This development is not just about power; it's about system-level efficiency," said Dr. Elena Vance, Chief Technical Officer at Global AutoSystems. "By operating at 800V, we significantly reduce ohmic losses throughout the vehicle's high-voltage bus, allowing for smaller wiring harnesses and faster DC charging capabilities."

Technical Specifications

The system features a peak output of 350kW and 4,500Nm of torque at the axle. Integration with our proprietary thermal management plate ensures sustained peak performance even under extreme towing or performance driving cycles.

Production is scheduled to commence at our Stuttgart Smart Factory in Q1 2027, with primary validation already completed in collaboration with three Tier-1 OEM partners.

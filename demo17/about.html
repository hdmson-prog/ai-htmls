Our Story

Engineering
Sovereignty

To provide the world's most discerning architects and home chefs with tools of absolute precision.

AETHERIA was born from a singular obsession: the perfection of thermal control. Today, we stand as the benchmark for luxury industrial design, combining Swiss engineering with global culinary insight.

1985

The First Foundry

Established as a precision metalworks in Zurich, specializing in high-tolerance components for the aerospace sector. This obsession with microns would later become the foundation of our cooking systems.

Thermal Breakthrough

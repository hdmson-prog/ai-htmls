About / Heritage

Industrial continuity shaped by engineering discipline

Founded to serve demanding automotive supply chains, Aurelium has evolved from precision bearing machining into a fully integrated wheel hub bearing manufacturer with dedicated lines for passenger vehicle, commercial vehicle, and tailored application programs.

Across three decades of expansion, the company has combined forging experience, CNC capability, controlled heat treatment, and digital inspection systems into a coherent manufacturing architecture built for consistency, scale, and traceability.

Manufacturing & Engineering

Each production stage is controlled for tolerance, stability, and repeatability

01

Forging

Controlled material selection and forging geometry establish dense grain structure and reliable mechanical strength from the outset.

02

CNC Machining

Multi-axis machining cells maintain dimensional accuracy and surface integrity for critical raceway and hub interfaces.

03

Heat Treatment

Programmable thermal cycles optimize hardness, fatigue resistance, and metallurgical consistency across high-volume batches.

04

Assembly

Clean assembly stations integrate seals, rolling elements, and lubricants with process-controlled torque and fit parameters.

05

Testing

Noise, vibration, durability, runout, and life-cycle validation confirm product readiness before release to shipment.

Quality & Certifications

Certified systems supported by layered inspection and validation

Production is governed by ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 quality frameworks, with process checkpoints spanning incoming material analysis, in-line dimensional inspection, metallographic verification, and final endurance validation.

Testing assets include roundness measurement, contour profiling, hardness analysis, salt spray review, fatigue simulation, noise evaluation, and assembly torque verification.

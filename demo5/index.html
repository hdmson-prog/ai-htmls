Heritage

Three Decades of Precision Manufacturing

Founded in 1994, Axiom Precision Components has evolved from a regional supplier to a global manufacturing partner for the automotive industry.

Our journey began with a singular focus: producing wheel hub bearings that meet the most demanding specifications in the industry. Today, we operate across three continents, serving OEM manufacturers and aftermarket distributors who share our commitment to quality.

Every bearing we produce carries the accumulated knowledge of nearly thirty years of engineering refinement. Our manufacturing processes have been developed through continuous improvement, guided by the principle that precision is not a goal but a requirement.

Process

Precision at Every Stage

Our manufacturing process integrates advanced technology with rigorous quality control, ensuring each bearing meets exact specifications.

01

Forging

Precision hot and cold forging creates the foundational geometry with optimal material grain structure for strength and durability.

02

CNC Machining

Multi-axis CNC machining achieves micron-level tolerances, ensuring perfect fit and function in every application.

03

Heat Treatment

Controlled atmosphere furnaces with advanced quenching systems deliver consistent hardness profiles and material properties.

04

Assembly

Automated assembly lines with vision systems ensure precise component positioning and preload calibration.

05

Testing

Every bearing undergoes comprehensive testing including dimensional inspection, rotational torque, and endurance evaluation.

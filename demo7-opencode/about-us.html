Our Story

From precision machining specialist to global automotive manufacturing partner

Apex began as a machining supplier serving regional vehicle programs and expanded into casting, assembly, and full program support as customer requirements shifted toward integrated, higher-volume manufacturing.

Today, our teams support product industrialization, tooling development, APQP, PPAP documentation, and launch management across passenger vehicle, commercial vehicle, and electrified platform programs.

  • Dedicated engineering teams for DFM, machining strategy, and quality planning
  • Serial production experience across ICE, hybrid, and EV platforms
  • Supply-chain coordination built around OEM timing and launch discipline
What We Deliver

Program discipline, repeatable quality, and regional responsiveness

Our production model focuses on manufacturing stability rather than one-off prototyping alone. Each program is supported with process validation, inspection planning, traceability, and escalation paths aligned to customer quality systems.

That structure allows us to scale responsibly from pilot phases into long-term supply while maintaining dimensional consistency, leak performance, and delivery reliability.

Product Description

These housings are designed to consolidate structural support, thermal management, and lubrication routing in one lightweight casting. Apex supports DFM optimization, tooling validation, serial-production control plans, and export packaging for multi-plant vehicle programs.

Critical bores, sealing faces, and mounting interfaces are finished with dedicated fixtures and monitored with CMM and inline gauging, enabling stable downstream assembly and leak performance.

Application

  • 8AT and 10AT passenger vehicle transmissions
  • Hybrid transaxles for PHEV and HEV platforms
  • Dedicated EV reducers and e-axle systems
  • Wet and dry dual-clutch transmissions
  • Commercial vehicle gearbox programs
  • Regional OEM and Tier-1 localization projects

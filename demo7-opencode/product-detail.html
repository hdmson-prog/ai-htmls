Overview

Built for thermal stability, sealing integrity, and high-volume repeatability

These housings integrate oil galleries, bearing seats, sensor bosses, and cooling interfaces in a single lightweight structure. Apex supports concept optimization, tooling development, PPAP submission, and global SOP ramp-up.

Leak-tested 100% Helium and air-decay options Machined datum traceability OEM launch support

Key Features

Production-ready design and process control

Integrated architecture

Supports fluid routing, mounting interfaces, and sensor integration while reducing secondary operations.

Stable machining datum strategy

Critical bores, bearing seats, and sealing faces are controlled with dedicated fixtures and in-process gauging.

Launch support for global programs

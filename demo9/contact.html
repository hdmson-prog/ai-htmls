Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery typically take?

Standard delivery times are 4-6 weeks for in-stock items. Custom orders may take 8-12 weeks depending on specifications. Express shipping is available for select products.

Do you offer design consultation services?

Yes, our design team offers complimentary consultations for projects. We can assist with product selection, space planning, and technical specifications.

What warranty do you provide?

All our products come with a comprehensive 10-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are available for commercial applications.

Can I order product samples?

Yes, we offer material samples and finish chips free of charge. Full product samples may be available for qualifying projects through our showrooms.

Do you work with architects and designers?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated trade program with exclusive benefits including priority support, project pricing, and technical resources.

Are your products sustainable?

Yes, all our products are manufactured using sustainable practices. We're ISO 14001 certified and many products qualify for LEED credits.

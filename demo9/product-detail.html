Premium Ceramic Craftsmanship

The Elegance Countertop Basin is manufactured using our proprietary ceramic blend, fired at precisely controlled temperatures exceeding 1250°C. This process creates a non-porous surface that is exceptionally durable and resistant to thermal shock.

Design Philosophy

Every curve and contour has been carefully considered to balance aesthetic beauty with practical functionality. The basin's generous depth provides ample water capacity while minimizing splash, and the smooth interior surface facilitates easy cleaning.

Advanced Glazing Technology

Multiple layers of specialized glaze are applied and fired separately, creating a surface that is:

  • Highly resistant to scratching and staining
  • Easy to clean with standard bathroom cleaners
  • Antibacterial and hygienic
  • UV-resistant to prevent yellowing over time

Professional Installation Recommended

While this basin can be installed by experienced DIYers, we recommend professional installation to ensure proper mounting and sealing.

Not Included (Sold Separately)

Installation Steps Overview

  1. Verify countertop is level and properly supported
  2. Position basin using included template
  3. Apply silicone sealant to basin bottom
  4. Set basin in position and secure
  5. Connect drain assembly and plumbing
  6. Apply final sealant bead around basin edge
  7. Allow 24 hours for sealant to cure before use

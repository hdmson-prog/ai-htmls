Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can book a consultation by filling out the contact form above, calling us directly, or visiting one of our showrooms. Our specialists will work with you to understand your needs and provide personalized recommendations.

What is included in a design consultation?

Our complimentary design consultations include a thorough assessment of your kitchen space, discussion of your cooking habits and preferences, product recommendations, technical specifications review, and integration planning with your existing or planned kitchen design.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, all Elementum appliances include professional white-glove installation by certified technicians. This includes delivery, installation, calibration, and in-home training on all features and functions.

What warranty coverage do you provide?

Every Elementum appliance comes with a comprehensive 5-year full warranty covering all parts and labor, plus an extended 10-year warranty on critical components. We also offer optional extended warranty plans.

Can I customize finishes and configurations?

Absolutely. We offer a range of standard finishes (Matte Black, Brushed Steel, Dark Copper, Champagne) and can arrange custom finishes for larger orders. Many of our appliances also offer configuration options to suit your specific needs.

How long does delivery take?

Standard products typically ship within 4-6 weeks. Custom configurations may take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

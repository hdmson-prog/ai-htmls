Engineering Excellence

Advanced Features

Precision Temperature Control

Multi-zone thermal sensors maintain accuracy within ±2°F. Advanced PID algorithms ensure consistent heat distribution across all cooking zones.

Steam Injection System

Programmable steam delivery creates the perfect crust on bread while maintaining moisture in meats. Professional-grade results at home.

Smart Cooking Programs

Over 100 pre-programmed cooking modes with AI-assisted temperature curves. Create and save custom profiles via the Elementum app.

Convection Excellence

Triple-fan convection system with reverse airflow mode. Eliminates hot spots and reduces cooking time by up to 25%.

Self-Cleaning Pyrolytic

High-temperature pyrolytic cleaning system reduces residue to ash. Triple-glazed door remains cool to touch during operation.

Telescopic Racks

Professional-grade soft-close telescopic racks with weight capacity up to 50 lbs. Multiple rack positions for versatile cooking.

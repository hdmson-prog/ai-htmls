Our Story

Excellence Through Innovation

Founded in 1999, Nexus Industrial began as a small precision machining shop with a vision to deliver world-class manufacturing solutions. Today, we've grown into a global manufacturing powerhouse, serving Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups alike.

Our journey has been defined by a relentless commitment to quality, continuous improvement, and deep partnerships with our clients. We don't just manufacture parts – we help bring ideas to life.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

1999

Company Founded

Nexus Industrial established with 20 employees and a single CNC machining center, focused on precision automotive components.

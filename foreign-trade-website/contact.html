Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical lead time?

Standard lead times range from 2-6 weeks depending on complexity and quantity. Rush orders may be available for urgent projects.

What is the minimum order quantity?

MOQ varies by product type, typically starting at 100-500 pieces. We also offer prototype quantities for new projects.

Do you offer custom manufacturing?

Yes! We specialize in OEM and ODM services. Our engineering team can help develop custom solutions to meet your specific requirements.

What shipping options are available?

We offer FOB, CIF, and DDP shipping terms with partners like DHL, FedEx, and sea freight for larger orders.

Can you provide samples?

Yes, we can provide samples for evaluation. Sample costs may apply but are often credited toward your first order.

What certifications do you hold?

We maintain ISO 9001, ISO 14001, IATF 16949, and AS9100D certifications among others. Products can be CE and UL certified as needed.

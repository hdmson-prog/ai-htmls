Atelier Introduces the Serie Noire Kitchen Collection

A darker, more architectural expression of the kitchen suite, developed for integrated homes and restrained interiors.

Atelier Home has introduced the Serie Noire kitchen collection, a new range of ovens, hobs, and cooling modules designed around lower visual contrast, flush installation, and a more monolithic architectural presence.

The collection extends the studio's existing approach to quiet performance by combining dark matte glass fronts, refined metal detailing, and interface decisions intended to recede into the wider room composition.

Design Direction

Rather than introducing dramatic gestures, the new range focuses on proportion, shadow lines, and finish coordination. The result is a kitchen system meant to support custom millwork, natural stone, and warm material palettes.

Dual convection cooking, steam assist functions, and improved energy calibration sit behind a deliberately restrained visual language, allowing technical capability to coexist with residential calm.

What the Launch Includes

The first wave of the collection includes the Serie Noire Oven, a new induction hob platform, and updated cooling modules intended for side-by-side architectural layouts. Additional laundry and air care expressions are scheduled to follow later in the year.

Atelier will present the collection through private showroom consultations and project-focused design reviews, with specification support available for architects, interior designers, and private clients.

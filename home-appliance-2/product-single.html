Overview

Precision for Daily Rituals

The Serie Noire Oven is designed for kitchens where technical performance and visual restraint carry equal weight. Dual convection ensures balanced heat circulation, while steam assist protects texture in breads, vegetables, and slow roasts.

The dark matte front panel, flush installation profile, and low-visibility handle detail allow the oven to sit quietly within millwork, making it suitable for refined residential and hospitality projects alike.

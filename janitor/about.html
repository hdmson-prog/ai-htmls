Our Story

Over three decades of innovation, craftsmanship, and excellence

Since 1990

Defining Excellence in Sanitary Ware

For more than three decades, Luxury Bath has been at the forefront of sanitary ware innovation, combining time-honored craftsmanship with cutting-edge manufacturing technology. What began as a vision to create the finest bathroom fixtures has evolved into a global presence, serving discerning clients across 85 countries.

We believe that a bathroom is more than a functional space—it's a sanctuary, a place of rejuvenation and personal expression. Every product we create reflects this philosophy, merging aesthetic sophistication with engineering precision to deliver solutions that elevate the everyday into the extraordinary.

Our Vision

To be the world's most respected manufacturer of luxury sanitary ware, recognized for uncompromising quality, timeless design, and sustainable innovation that enhances lives and spaces globally.

Our Mission

To craft exceptional bathroom solutions through meticulous engineering, sustainable practices, and unwavering attention to detail, empowering architects, designers, and homeowners to realize their vision of the perfect space.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1990

Foundation

Luxury Bath established with a single manufacturing facility and a commitment to redefining quality in sanitary ware.

1998

International Expansion

Opened first international showroom in Milan, Italy. Began exporting to European luxury hospitality projects.

2005

Innovation Award

Received prestigious International Design Excellence Award (IDEA) for our Signature Basin Collection.

2012

Sustainability Commitment

Achieved ISO 14001 certification. Launched water-saving intelligent toilet systems reducing consumption by 50%.

2018

Global Network

Expanded to 85 countries with 12 manufacturing facilities and 200+ authorized distributors worldwide.

2026

Future Forward

Continuing to innovate with smart technology integration and carbon-neutral manufacturing initiatives.

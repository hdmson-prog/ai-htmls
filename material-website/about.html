Who We Are

Leading Innovation in Brazing Materials

Precision Materials Co. is a globally recognized manufacturer of premium industrial brazing materials and thermal joining solutions. With over 25 years of experience, we've established ourselves as the trusted partner for manufacturers worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer success has made us the preferred choice for industries ranging from HVAC and automotive to aerospace and industrial equipment manufacturing. We combine advanced metallurgy with precision engineering to deliver products that exceed international standards.

Every product that leaves our facility represents our dedication to excellence, backed by rigorous quality control, comprehensive testing, and continuous improvement processes.

Our Mission

To provide world-class brazing materials and thermal joining solutions that enable our customers to achieve manufacturing excellence, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in advanced material solutions, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to helping manufacturers build better products that improve lives around the world.

Our Values

Quality without compromise, innovation through research, customer success as our priority, sustainability in everything we do, and integrity in all our relationships.

Usage Guidelines

Surface Preparation

  1. Clean all surfaces thoroughly to remove oils, grease, and contaminants
  2. Remove oxide layers using mechanical or chemical methods
  3. Ensure proper fit-up with joint clearances of 0.05-0.20mm
  4. Apply flux evenly if required (consult technical support)

Brazing Process

  1. Preheat assembly uniformly to 500-550°C
  2. Apply filler rod when base metal reaches brazing temperature
  3. Heat to 590-610°C for optimal flow
  4. Allow filler to flow into joint by capillary action
  5. Remove heat source when joint is filled
  6. Cool gradually to room temperature

Safety Precautions

  • Work in well-ventilated area
  • Wear appropriate PPE (gloves, safety glasses, protective clothing)
  • Keep fire extinguisher nearby
  • Follow all local safety regulations

Storage Recommendations

  • Store in cool, dry location
  • Keep away from moisture and corrosive materials
  • Maintain original packaging until use
  • Shelf life: 24 months under proper storage conditions

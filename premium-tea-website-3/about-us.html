Our
Story.

From ancient mountains to your cup — a journey of reverence, patience, and uncompromising quality.

Our
Mission.

Míng Lù exists to preserve and celebrate the art of exceptional tea cultivation. We believe that great tea is born from deep respect — for the land, the seasons, and the families who have stewarded these ancient gardens for generations.

In a world of mass production and compromise, we stand firm: single-origin. Small harvest. No shortcuts. Every tea we offer represents a conversation between soil, sky, and time — a conversation we're privileged to share with you.

Our
Values

🌍

Authenticity

Direct relationships with farming families. No middlemen, no blending, no compromise. Each tea tells the story of its origin.

⏳

Patience

Great tea cannot be rushed. We wait for the perfect harvest window, the precise moment when quality reaches its peak.

🌿

Sustainability

We're committed to preserving the mountain ecosystems that produce our teas. Stewardship of the land is non-negotiable.

💎

Excellence

In every aspect—from sourcing to packaging to service—we pursue the highest standards. Anything less is not Míng Lù.

Our
Journey

2015

The Beginning

A journey begins in the mist-covered mountains of Yunnan, inspired by generations of tea mastery and a desire to share something truly exceptional.

2017

First Partnerships

We establish direct relationships with five family gardens across Yunnan, Zhejiang, and Jiangsu. Trust is built over time.

2019

Quality First

We turn down over 70% of harvests that don't meet our standards. Our commitment to excellence becomes legendary among growers.

2021

Global Community

Míng Lù tea reaches connoisseurs in over 30 countries. Our community of tea lovers grows daily.

2024

Today

We continue our mission: to honor ancient traditions, support farming families, and share the transformative power of exceptional tea.

Direct
Relationships

We don't buy from brokers. We visit the gardens. We know the families. We understand their challenges, celebrate their successes, and commit to fair prices that honor their craft.

This approach means we work with fewer gardens, source smaller quantities, and maintain absolute control over quality. It also means we can tell you exactly where your tea comes from and who grew it.

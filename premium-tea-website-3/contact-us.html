Frequently
Asked Questions

What does "single-origin" mean?

Each tea comes from a specific garden or region, never blended with teas from elsewhere. This allows us to preserve the unique character of each tea's terroir.

How do I know which tea to choose?

Visit our Products page for detailed descriptions of each tea. We recommend starting with Lóng Jǐng if you prefer lighter, more delicate teas, or Gyokuro for something deeper and more umami-rich.

Do you offer samples?

Yes! We offer sample packs so you can explore our collection. Contact us for details and pricing on our sample program.

What's your shipping policy?

We ship worldwide via DHL Express. Packaging is designed to preserve tea quality and freshness. Typical delivery is 7-14 business days.

How should I store my tea?

Store in a cool, dry place in an airtight container. Avoid exposure to light, strong odors, and humidity. Properly stored tea can maintain quality for years.

Are you available for wholesale?

Absolutely! We work with tea shops, restaurants, and specialty retailers. Contact us to discuss wholesale opportunities and pricing.

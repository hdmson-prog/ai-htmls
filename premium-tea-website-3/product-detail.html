About This Tea

Lóng Jǐng, or Dragon Well, represents one of China's most celebrated green teas. Our Pre-Qīngmíng selection is harvested in the fleeting days before the Qīngmíng festival — a window of mere days that captures the finest leaves at their peak of delicate complexity.

Pressed into distinctive flat, sword-like shapes, these leaves unfurl to reveal a pale, luminous liquor of remarkable clarity. The first infusion brings notes of fresh chestnuts and spring blossoms, evolving into deeper mineral notes reminiscent of wet stones after rain.

Our Lóng Jǐng comes from family gardens in the West Lake region of Hangzhou, where tea cultivation has flourished for over 1,200 years. The unique microclimate — cool mornings, afternoon mist, and mineral-rich soils — creates conditions ideally suited to producing teas of unparalleled complexity.

We work directly with the farming families, many of whom have stewarded these specific plots for generations. Each harvest is carefully monitored to ensure we select only the most prized picking window — the pre-Qīngmíng period — capturing leaves at their absolute peak of refinement.

Our Story

From Passion to Purpose

Founded in 2010, Lumière began as a small workshop with a simple mission: to create candles that transform spaces into sanctuaries. What started as a passion project by artisan candlemaker Marie Laurent has grown into an internationally recognized brand, beloved for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and artistry.

Every candle we create is handcrafted with premium, natural ingredients and infused with carefully curated fragrances. Our dedication to excellence has earned us partnerships with luxury hotels, spas, and discerning customers worldwide who appreciate the art of fine candle making.

Marie Laurent

Founder & Master Candlemaker

Our Values

What We Stand For

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials, renewable soy wax, and recyclable packaging to minimize our environmental impact.

Quality

Every candle undergoes rigorous testing to ensure consistent burn quality, fragrance throw, and safety standards.

Craftsmanship

Each candle is hand-poured by skilled artisans who take pride in creating products of exceptional beauty and quality.

Community

We support local communities and fair trade practices, ensuring ethical sourcing and positive social impact.

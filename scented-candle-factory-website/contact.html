FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your candles made of?

Our candles are made with 100% natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and cotton wicks. All ingredients are non-toxic and eco-friendly.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact us at wholesale@lumiere.com for more information.

Can I create a custom scent?

Absolutely! We offer custom fragrance development services. Contact our team to discuss your vision and requirements.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on orders over $75 within the US. International shipping is available. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, contact us for a full refund or exchange.

Are your candles cruelty-free?

Yes, all our products are 100% cruelty-free and vegan. We never test on animals and use only plant-based ingredients.

Our Story

Crafting Memories Through Fragrance

Founded in 2010, Lumière has been at the forefront of luxury candle manufacturing, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation. Our passion lies in creating extraordinary sensory experiences that elevate everyday moments into cherished memories.

Each candle is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and artistic excellence. We source the finest natural waxes, essential oils, and materials from around the world, ensuring every product meets our exacting standards.

Pure, Natural Ingredients

We believe luxury shouldn't come at the expense of our environment. That's why every Lumière candle is crafted using only the finest natural, sustainable ingredients.

Our commitment extends beyond our products to our packaging, operations, and partnerships. We work exclusively with suppliers who share our values of environmental stewardship and ethical sourcing.

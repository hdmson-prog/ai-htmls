The Journey to 100% Sustainable Candle Production

Marie Laurent Founder & CEO
December 15, 2023 8 min read 2,345 views
Sustainability Eco-Friendly Innovation

In 2023, Lumière achieved a milestone we've been working towards for years: 100% sustainable candle production. This transformation wasn't just about meeting environmental standards—it was about reimagining every aspect of how we create our beloved candles.

The Beginning of Our Sustainability Journey

When I founded Lumière in 2010, sustainability was already at the core of our mission. However, as we grew, we realized that true sustainability required more than good intentions—it demanded comprehensive action across every stage of production.

Our journey began with a simple question: "Can we create luxury candles that are completely carbon-neutral?" The answer, we discovered, was yes—but it would require innovation, investment, and unwavering commitment.

"Sustainability isn't a destination; it's a continuous journey of improvement and innovation. Every decision we make considers its impact on our planet and future generations." - Marie Laurent, Founder

Key Initiatives in Our Transformation

1. Renewable Soy Wax

We transitioned from traditional paraffin wax to 100% renewable soy wax sourced from certified sustainable farms. This change alone reduced our carbon footprint by 65% and provided a cleaner, longer-lasting burn for our customers.

2. Zero-Waste Packaging

Every piece of packaging is now recyclable, compostable, or reusable. Our signature boxes are made from post-consumer recycled paper, and we've eliminated all plastic from our shipping materials.

3. Renewable Energy

Our production facility now runs entirely on solar and wind power. We installed 500 solar panels on our roof and partnered with local wind farms to ensure a consistent supply of renewable energy.

4. Ethical Sourcing

All our fragrance oils are sourced from suppliers who meet strict ethical and environmental standards. We work directly with farmers and distilleries that practice sustainable harvesting and fair labor practices.

The Impact So Far

Environmental Impact

  • Reduced carbon emissions by 85%
  • Eliminated 50 tons of plastic waste annually
  • Preserved 10,000 trees through sustainable sourcing
  • Achieved carbon-neutral certification

The response from our customers has been overwhelmingly positive. Not only do they appreciate our commitment to the planet, but they've also noticed improvements in burn quality, scent throw, and overall product experience.

Looking Forward

While achieving 100% sustainable production is a major milestone, our work is far from over. We're now focused on:

  • Expanding our renewable energy initiatives
  • Developing biodegradable wick materials
  • Creating a candle recycling program
  • Partnering with reforestation projects
  • Educating our industry peers on sustainable practices

Join Us on This Journey

Sustainability is a collective effort. Every candle you purchase supports our mission to protect the planet while bringing beauty and fragrance into your home. Together, we can prove that luxury and sustainability are not mutually exclusive—they're complementary.

We invite you to be part of this journey. Share your thoughts, ask questions, and help us continue improving. Because at Lumière, we believe the best way to predict the future is to create it—sustainably.

Heritage & Legacy

In the heart of Grasse, where perfumery tradition runs through generations, our manufactory was born from a profound respect for the olfactory arts.

For over a century, we have refined the delicate balance between ancestral knowledge and modern precision. Our atelier remains committed to the patient craftsmanship that cannot be rushed—hand-pouring each candle, blending fragrances with the same meticulous care our founders employed in 1892.

We do not follow trends. We preserve a lineage of excellence that serves those who understand the value of time, tradition, and uncompromising quality.

Craftsmanship

The Manufactory Process

01

Fragrance Composition

Master perfumers collaborate with our fragrance library, developed over 130 years. Each composition balances top, heart, and base notes with architectural precision.

02

Wax Formulation

Natural wax blends are formulated to achieve optimal fragrance diffusion, burn quality, and vessel compatibility. We work exclusively with sustainable sources.

03

Hand Pouring

Each candle is poured by trained artisans who understand the precise temperature and timing required for flawless results. This cannot be automated.

04

Quality Assurance

Rigorous testing protocols ensure consistent performance. Every batch undergoes burn testing, scent throw evaluation, and safety certification.

Responsibility

Sustainability & Ethics

Our commitment to the environment extends beyond compliance. It is woven into our production philosophy.

Natural Materials

We source renewable waxes—soy, coconut, and beeswax—from certified sustainable farms. No paraffin. No petroleum derivatives.

Clean Combustion

Cotton wicks free from lead or zinc. Phthalate-free fragrances. Every component tested for indoor air quality standards.

Circular Design

Vessels designed for reuse. Packaging materials recyclable or biodegradable. Minimal waste in production processes.

Certifications

ISO 9001, REACH compliant, Ecocert verified. Our standards exceed regulatory requirements across all markets.

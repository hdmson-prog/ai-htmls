About Verdant

A tea house shaped by mountain weather, patience, and old craft.

Verdant Tea partners with estates, tea makers, and global buyers who value origin, clarity, and a quietly luxurious drinking experience.

Our Philosophy

We treat tea as an agricultural art, not a commodity line.

From the first spring harvest to the final packed carton, every decision is made to preserve the identity of the leaf. We work with mountain-grown teas that carry distinct aroma, shape, and cup structure, then build export-ready programs around that character rather than diluting it.

Our collections are designed for partners who want more than generic supply. We help tea rooms, specialty retail, hospitality groups, and private label brands source teas that feel rooted, elegant, and commercially dependable.

"Quietly refined tea, prepared for modern trade."

Our Journey

A brief timeline of Verdant Tea

1892

Mountain beginnings

Our story begins with hillside plantings cultivated in mist-rich terrain known for slow leaf development and aromatic depth.

1958

Craft refined for export

Processing standards were formalized for consistency, allowing traditional styles to travel well into international tea programs.

2008

Private label expansion

Verdant began offering branded packaging and tailored collections for specialty retailers and boutique hospitality clients.

Today

Heritage with precision

We continue to combine origin-led sourcing with modern documentation, packaging support, and export coordination.

Our Values

What defines the Verdant standard

Origin First

Long-Term Trust

We value stable partnerships, honest specifications, and responsive communication over one-off transactions.

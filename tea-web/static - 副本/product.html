Chunmee Reserve is a classic eyebrow-style green tea selected from early spring flushes and crafted for clarity, consistency, and a polished cup profile. The leaf presents a fine curve with subtle silver-green tone and opens into a bright liquor with chestnut sweetness, gentle nuttiness, and a clean finish.

This lot is positioned for buyers who need an approachable premium green tea that balances heritage character with scalable supply. It performs well in loose leaf tins, refill programs, food service menus, and export assortments where visual leaf identity matters.

  • Harvest season: early spring, first tender leaf sets.
  • Processing style: pan-fixed, rolled, and slow dried for fragrance retention.
  • Cup profile: chestnut, fresh greens, soft sweetness, low astringency.

Chunmee Reserve is suitable across retail, hospitality, and private label channels. Its stable cup profile and distinctive leaf shape make it useful for both premium storytelling and repeat purchase programs.

  • Loose leaf retail in tins, stand-up pouches, and gift cartons.
  • Hotel and tea room service where visual dry leaf presentation matters.
  • Foodservice brewing for hot tea menus and light cold brew programs.
  • Private label export collections paired with regional or seasonal assortments.

Recommended brewing for trade sampling is 3 grams per 200ml at 80-85C for 2 minutes, with a second infusion to demonstrate structure and consistency.

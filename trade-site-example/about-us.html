Our Story

Built around discipline, not noise

CopperForge started with one objective: manufacture fittings that installers can trust without rework, guesswork, or repeat visits. The company grew by focusing on process control, material verification, and responsive export support instead of chasing volume alone.

Today we serve procurement teams, distributors, and OEM partners with repeatable batches, clear documentation, and production lines tuned for stable output.

What we value

ConsistencyEvery batch is checked against the same dimensional, visual, and pressure criteria.
ResponsivenessQuotation, sampling, and technical support stay fast enough for active supply programs.
AccountabilityTraceability and documentation are part of the release process, not an afterthought.
